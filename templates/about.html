    About

    The Good News Aggregator is a web application that scrapes news articles from various news websites that specifically publish news that is considered good/positive/uplifting. With much of the world focusing on negative storylines, this application aims to remind us of the great things happening in the world every day. All the scraped articles come from the websites below:

    • https://www.thehappybroadcast.com/
    • https://www.goodnewsnetwork.org/category/news/usa/
    • https://www.positive.news/articles/
    • https://www.sunnyskyz.com/good-news
    • https://www.huffpost.com/impact/topic/good-news
    • https://www.today.com/news/good-news

    This project serves as my final project for CS50x, an online course I took in the summer of 2019 to learn the fundamentals of computer science. Throughout this project, I've had the opportunity to explore web development, web scraping, and database management.
